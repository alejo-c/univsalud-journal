Articles published in Universidad y Salud are indexed or summarized by:

Indexing directories

DOAJ		 Directory of Open Access Journals es un directorio de revistas de acceso abierto, proporciona un acceso libre y completo a artículos de revistas revisadas por pares en diversas disciplinas.

Latin REV		 Latin American Network of Academic Journals in Social Sciences and Humanities, which groups academic journals from Latin America, focusing on the dissemination and promotion of research in social sciences and humanities.

REDIB		 Ibero-American Network for Innovation and Scientific Knowledge, a platform that integrates scientific and academic journals from Ibero-America, promoting open access and visibility of scientific knowledge in Spanish and Portuguese
Citation indexes

MIAR		 Information Matrix for the Analysis of Journals, which provides data on scientific journals, including their characteristics, indexes, and quality criteria, particularly in the Hispanic context.

LATINDEX		 Regional System of Information for Scientific Journals from Latin America, the Caribbean, Spain and Portugal, which provides information on scientific journals in the region, with the aim of improving their visibility and access

LILACS		 Latin American and Caribbean Health Sciences Literature, a database that brings together scientific and technical health literature from Latin America and the Caribbean, promoting visibility and access to relevant research.
Fulltext content agregators

SciELO Colombia		 Scientific Electronic Library Online - Colombia, a platform offering access to scientific journals and articles online, focused on Colombia’s scientific production
Bibliographic databases

Google Académico		 Search engine indexing academic literature from various disciplines, offering access to articles, theses, books, and conferences, both open and restricted access

Crossref		 An organization providing digital object identifiers (DOIs) for academic articles, facilitating reference and citation of research

Dialnet		 A portal for the dissemination of Spanish-language scientific production, including articles, theses, and other academic documents, mainly in Spanish

Open Alex		 Academic database that facilitates the discovery and access to articles, authors, and publications, promoting open access.
Others

Scilit		 A database indexing academic literature and allowing users to explore articles and publications in an accessible, structured way.

SIRED		 The Institutional Digital Resources System of the University of Nariño groups and manages academic digital resources, facilitating access to publications, research, and other materials

EZB		 Electronic Journals Library is a platform that offers access to electronic journals, facilitating the search and consultation of publications in various knowledge areas

BVS 		Virtual Health Library is an information network providing access to health resources, promoting the dissemination of scientific knowledge in public health.

OAJIF 		The Open Access Journals Impact Factor (OAJIF) is an international scientific research organisation that provides indexing of major international journals and proceedings.

DARDO 		Dardo is a system for publishing calls for papers (CFP) from open access scientific journals. Its aim is to disseminate serial publications and provide researchers with accurate and reliable information on where to submit their manuscripts.
